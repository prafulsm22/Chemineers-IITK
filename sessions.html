ACTIVITIES


Departmental Freshers' Event

The society likes to welcome the freshers of the CHE department each year with an enthralling and informative freshers' event. The society organizes a tour of the CHE department for the freshers which includes visiting the various labs and having some informal talks with the professors of the department to get a feel of it. Ofcourse, the event ends with a hearty feast and some fun-filled competitions !

Departmental Farewell

Just as we like to welcome our freshers, we also like to bid a proper goodbye to our graduating students who leave with a happy memory of the 4 years they spent on campus. Profesors also give some words of advice and encouragement as the graduating students prepare to make their way into the world. The junior members of the society and the department organise fun sessions and we also hand out some parting goodies as mementos.



Higher Studies Sessions

Many students wish to pursue higher studies after graduating. To help them with their endeavours, the society invites alumni who pursued higher studies in diverse domains to share their experience and impart guidance to the students. Sometimes, professors are also invited to help the students make an informed decision. These sessions have been quite helpful over the past few years and the number of students from the department taking an interest in higher studies has grown steadily over the years.

Intern Sessions

The society works closely with the Student Placement Office and the Academic and Career Council to attract companies especially in the Core domain. Only a small fraction of students are able to get an internship opportunity through SPO. The majority of Junta go through off-campus opportunities. Finding these off-campus opportunities sometimes becomes tedious and time-consuming. To speed-up the process and make it easier, the society approaches various companies to provide internship opportunities to the students at a reasonable stipend.



Intern Connect

Internships are of utmost importance during our college years. They provide exposure to the real world. One of the most valuable skills we gain from an internship is the ability to speak with people in a professional setting. They help us to learn more about ourselves and gain a competitive edge. The society conducts ConnectWithIntern sessions where students can get all their doubts cleared by some of the seniors of CHE department who have done internship in Core and Non-Core domains.

Happy Hours

These cover a variety of events whose main aim is to develop a sense of bonding among the students of the department and also between the students and professors. Regular informal meetings are held where various Professors from the department are invited so that students could know about their work, area of research etc. Fun-filled sports competitions and quizes are also organised for the students to ignite their team and competitive spirits.
